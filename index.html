01 — What is an ETA?

A prediction.
Not a promise.

An ETA is our best estimate of when a vehicle arrives. It shapes customer expectations — and drives fleet activations.

01 — Our Mission

The most realistic
prediction possible.

Uncertain → show a wide range
Don't suggest certainty where there isn't any.
−4 wks Most Likely ETA +4 wks
Confident → show a narrow range
Signal precision where the data supports it.
−4 wks Most Likely ETA +4 wks

Target: maximum efficient activations · no unnecessary customer delays

01 — Limitations

What we can't
fully control.

Production disruptions — strikes or part shortages at OEM plants
Blocked logistics routes — Suez, Red Sea, or other sea route disruptions
Limited OEM data — not every manufacturer shares real-time production status

Global supply chains are inherently unpredictable. Our role: be as accurate as possible within those constraints.
